How It Works

Seven steps.
Workflow.

A tight, transparent work process that keeps you in control without drowning you in decisions.

01
Consultation/Discovery Call
Your product, your audience, what this video must accomplish. No fluff — total clarity on the goal before a single pixel moves.
02
Moodboarding
Gathering visual inspiration and mood to set the tone for the project.
03
Storyboarding
Story mapped, visuals locked. You review and approve before a single frame is animated. No surprises mid-project.
04
Styleframes
Creating style guides and frames to establish the visual style.
05
Animation Phase
Every scene built on-brand, polished, and purposeful. This is where the work becomes extraordinary.
06
Sound design
Adding audio elements to enhance the animation.
07
Feedback/Delivery
Clean source files and exports, on time. Revision rounds built in. Ready to publish the moment it lands in your inbox.
